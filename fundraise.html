Fundraise for Us

What is Fundraising?

Fundraising is a method of raising money that encourages supporters to utilize their own network to raise money on behalf of an organization. Supporters use an online platform to engage their friends, family, co-workers, and neighbours to donate to a fundraising page that directs funds to the non-profit organization.


Why Fundraise for Us?

By fundraising for our organization, you're contributing to our efforts to provide education, healthcare, clean water, and community development programs to those in need. Your fundraising efforts will directly impact the lives of individuals and families in Mukusu Gomba Village, Uganda.


Why start a fundraising campaign?

A fundraising campaign for Jodrine Foundation is the best way for an individual to increase their impact for an organization (especially for those who may not be able to make a large personal donation). Starting a fundraising campaign is an easy and effective way to raise money to provide help and hope to neighbours that need it most. Individuals, corporations, community groups and others can create their own teams and help raise valuable support for those in need.
