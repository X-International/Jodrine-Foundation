Support Our Mission

Support Our Initiatives

Your contribution can make a significant difference in the lives of individuals and communities served by the Jodrine Foundation. By donating today, you're helping us advance our mission of empowering communities, promoting education, and fostering sustainable development. Together, we can create lasting change and build a brighter future for all.



How Your Donation Helps

Education: Providing access to quality education, school infrastructure, and learning resources.

Healthcare: Improving access to healthcare services, medical facilities, and preventive care.

Water Projects: Installing wells, boreholes, and water purification systems to ensure clean and safe drinking water for communities.

Economic Empowerment: Supporting entrepreneurship, skills development, and livelihood opportunities for individuals and families.

Impact of Your Donation

Learn about the impact your donation can have on the lives of individuals and communities:


One-Time Donation Tiers:

$26: Your $26 donation will help provide essential school supplies such as textbooks, pencils, and notebooks for one child at Jodrine Junior School, ensuring they have the tools they need to succeed in their education.

$53: With your $53 donation, we can fund a portion of the ongoing construction of a new well in Mukusu village, providing clean and accessible water to the community and improving the health and well-being of its residents.

$170: Your generous $170 donation will go towards covering medical expenses for Muwanguzi Nicholas, enabling him to receive the necessary treatment to fix his leg and improve his mobility, transforming his life and giving him hope for a brighter future.



Monthly Donation Tiers:

$8 per month: By pledging just $8 per month, you can contribute towards the daily nutritious meals provided to the children at Jodrine Junior School, ensuring they have the energy and focus to thrive in their studies.

$26 per month: Your monthly donation of $26 will support the salary of dedicated volunteers like Viene, Resty, and Harriet, who play vital roles in educating and nurturing the children at Jodrine Junior School, making a lasting impact on their lives.

$53 per month: With your monthly donation of $53, we can continue to expand our educational programs and facilities at Jodrine Junior School, providing more children in Mukusu village with access to quality education and a brighter future.



Yearly Donation Tiers:

$53 per year: Your yearly donation of $53 will contribute towards the maintenance and improvement of the school infrastructure at Jodrine Junior School, creating a safe and conducive learning environment for all students.

$170 per year: With your annual donation of $170, we can invest in additional resources and extracurricular activities, such as sports equipment and music instruments, enriching the educational experience of the children at Jodrine Junior School.

$260 per year: Your generous yearly donation of $260 will support the ongoing efforts to build a nearby hospital in Mukusu village, providing critical healthcare services to the community and saving lives in times of need.
