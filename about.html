About Jodrine Foundation

Together, We
Can Change Lives

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple yet profound desire to make a difference in the lives of underserved communities. Founded by Namata Joyce and Kalule Edrine, our organization is rooted in a deep commitment to education, healthcare, and community development. Inspired by the challenges faced by individuals like Muwanguzi Nicholas, a young boy with disabilities who traversed long distances to access education, we set out to create positive change in Mukusu, Gomba and beyond.

Empowering Futures

Our mission is simple yet profound: to inspire transformation, one life at a time. We envision a future where every child has access to quality education, every family has access to healthcare, and every individual has access to clean water. Through our work, we strive to create a ripple effect of positive change that extends far beyond our village.

Community-Led Development

We believe in a holistic approach to community development that addresses the root causes of poverty and inequality. Through strategic partnerships, community engagement, and innovative solutions, we work tirelessly to implement projects that improve access to education, healthcare, clean water, and economic opportunities.

Key Focus Areas

Education

We are dedicated to expanding access to quality education for all children, regardless of socioeconomic status or ability. Our initiatives include the establishment of Jodrine Junior School, provision of scholarships, and support for educational resources and infrastructure.

Clean Water

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right that underpins health and economic opportunity. Through water projects and hygiene education, we strive to provide communities with sustainable access to safe drinking water and promote healthy sanitation practices.

Healthcare

Ensuring access to affordable and quality healthcare services is essential for community well-being. We advocate for improved healthcare infrastructure, preventive healthcare programs, and access to essential medical services for underserved populations.

Our Core Values

Guiding Principles

Empowerment: We believe in empowering individuals and communities to reach their full potential and fostering self-reliance.

Inclusivity: We are dedicated to inclusivity and equity, ensuring that everyone, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to essential resources and opportunities.

Integrity: Integrity is at the core of everything we do. We are transparent, accountable, and ethical in our actions, earning the trust and respect of our stakeholders.

Collaboration: We recognize the power of collaboration and partnership in creating meaningful change. We work closely with local communities, government agencies and other stakeholders to leverage resources and expertise for greater impact.

Innovation: We embrace innovation and creativity as catalysts for progress. We continuously seek new and effective solutions to address the evolving needs of the communities we serve.

Compassion: At the heart of Jodrine Foundation's work is a deep sense of compassion for those in need. We believe in treating every individual with kindness, dignity, and respect, and strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment for all.

Our Achievements

Milestones That Define Us

Establishment of Jodrine Junior School

Since its inception, Jodrine Foundation has successfully established Jodrine Junior School, providing access to quality education for children in Mukusu, Gomba. Through dedicated efforts and community support, the school has become a beacon of hope and opportunity for young learners in the area.

Clean Water Initiatives

Jodrine Foundation has initiated clean water projects aimed at providing sustainable access to safe drinking water for communities in need. With the construction of the first well underway, the organization is poised to make significant strides in improving public health and sanitation in the region.
