Join Us Today

Join Us Today

Welcome to the Jodrine Foundation's Membership Program! By becoming a member, you're joining a community dedicated to transforming lives in Mukusu Gomba village through education, healthcare, and community development initiatives. Explore our membership tiers below and choose the one that best aligns with your commitment to positive change.



Why Join the Membership Program:

Your support as a member is vital to our work in Mukusu Gomba village. By joining us, you'll be making a tangible difference in the lives of those we serve, helping to provide education, healthcare, and opportunities for a brighter future.



Membership Tiers

Monthly Membership Tiers

Learning Advocate: With a monthly contribution of $8, you get monthly updates on education initiatives and access to exclusive Webinars on Healthcare in Mukusu village.

Health Hero: With a monthly contribution of $26, You have access to Learning Advocate's Membership benefits plus Quarterly Health Reports on Mukusu Gomba Community and Recognition on Our Website as a Health Hero.

Community Champion: With a monthly contribution of $53, You have access to Health Hero Membership benefits plus Priority Access to Community Development Projects Updates and Invitation to Annual Community Gathering Event.



Annual Membership Tiers

Education Ambassador: With an annual contribution of $170, you get Quarterly Reports on Educational Progress in Mukusu Gomba and Exclusive Access to Virtual Tours of School Projects.

Healthcare Patron: With an annual contribution of $260, you get access to all Education Ambassador Membership benefits plus a Personalized Thank-You Video from Beneficiary Families and VIP Invitation to Healthcare Workshops in Mukusu Gomba.

Community Leader: With an annual contribution of $800, you get access to all Healthcare Patron Membership benefits plus Quarterly Virtual Meetings with Foundation Founders and Opportunity to Sponsor a Community Development Project in Mukusu Gomba.



Ready to Make a Difference?

Choose your membership tier and join us in creating a brighter future for Mukusu Gomba village. Your support empowers us to continue our vital work and bring positive change to those in need. Together, we can make a meaningful impact on the lives of others.
