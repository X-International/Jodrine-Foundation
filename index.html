Who We Are

Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives

Welcome to the Jodrine Foundation, a beacon of hope in Mukusu Gomba village. Founded with a vision to empower and transform lives, we are committed to making a tangible difference in our community through education, healthcare, and access to clean water.

Our foundation is built upon a solid foundation of values, including integrity, compassion, and community empowerment. We believe in transparency, accountability, and collaboration as we work towards our shared goals. Every decision we make is guided by these principles, ensuring that our actions align with our mission and vision.

Since our inception, we have made significant strides in improving the lives of individuals and communities in Mukusu Gomba and beyond. From starting Jodrine junior school, to implementing our first clean water project, our impact is tangible and transformative.

EDUCATION
Education Initiatives

Education lies at the heart of our mission. We believe that every child deserves access to quality education, regardless of their circumstances. Through our dedication and efforts, we have initiated the Jodrine Junior School in the village of Mukusu Gomba, laying the foundation for a brighter future for the children in our community.

HEALTH
Healthcare Programs

Access to quality healthcare is a fundamental right that many in the village of Mukusu lack. We are committed to addressing this critical need and improving healthcare outcomes for our community. We recognize that the well-being of our residents hinges on access to essential medical services.
